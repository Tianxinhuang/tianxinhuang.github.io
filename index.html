Biography [CV]

I am currently a Postdoc Fellow in the University of Hong Kong (HKU), School of Computing and Data Science (CDS) and Institute of Data Science (IDS), working with Prof. Shenghua Gao and Prof. Yi Ma . Before that, I was a Research Fellow (Postdoc) in National University of Singapore (NUS), School of Computing, with Prof. Gim Hee Lee . I earned my Doctor's Degree at April Lab, Zhejiang University (ZJU), under the supervision of Prof. Yong Liu. I received the Bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU) in 2017. My esearch interests includes but not limited on:

3D Geometry Analysis, 3D Human Face Modeling, 3D Scene Understanding, 3D/4D World Generation
